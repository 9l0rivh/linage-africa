About Lineage Africa

Linage Africa Limited was established in Tanzania in 2023 with its office domiciled on the 12th Floor,New Bagamoyo road, Mwanga Towers in Dar Es Salaam.
The core business for Linage Africa Limited is Transportation and Logistical services for both petroleum products and dry shipments in Tanzania as well as cross border destinations.
The directors have vast experience in Transport and Logistical services having worked for some of main Logistical companies including top shipping companies hence being exposed to international engagements.
